About

Making Your Life Count is a cooperative effort of the following three.

Coaching Team

We want you to know that you are not alone if you choose to personally join the quest to make your life count for more. In addition to offering the free short ebook, interactive email series, online lessons and new assessment tools to guide you through developing and applying WALK, PRAY, CARE and SHARE in your life, you have an entire team of qualified coaches ready to assist you with personalized input throughout your journey to increase the positive effect you make in your and others’ worlds.

Radio

The Making Your Life Count radio program, originally known as The Lighthouse Report, has been broadcast to millions for many years running now. Each short yet packed program offers practical, relevant approaches and ideas through interviews with people just like you who are making their lives count for more. These ideas are designed to both inspire and help you to extend your influence for Christ to your extended communities.

Cru.org

Our parent organization, Cru, is a caring community passionate about connecting an ever-growing number of people to share in the joy of having lives of faith. Lives that make a difference. You can increasingly develop into one who grows in WALK, PRAY, CARE and SHARE—and also guide others like yourself to do the same. The newly redesigned site Cru.org offers a substantial amount of helpful and diverse additional resources for you to explore.
