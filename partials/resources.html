Resources

You can use the following grid to help you quickly get to the best resource for your need. Across the top you see our four elements of WALK - PRAY - CARE - SHARE. There are then three tiers of resources:

Our Recommendations: These links will take you directly to the interactive lesson for each of the four themes. These four are ones where we can see measurable results, are focused on the topic and have been proven to work.

Endorsed Partner Tools: These have the same three qualifications; measurable, focused, proven. These are regularly rotated out as we find others that are more specific to our desired outcome.

Other Ideas: Submitted by others but matching our desired guidelines. They meet a basic set of guidelines.
